<p>The Values in Practice (VIP) Programme was introduced in 2011 as one of the key character development programmes as part of our efforts in nurturing our students into leaders of character who champion service and excellence.</p><p>The VIP Programme aims to:</p><ul data-tight="true" class="tight"><li><p>Provide platforms for students to demonstrate the school values which are also the CCE Core values (R3ICH) in tandem with MOE's direction of providing students with Student-Centric Values-Driven Education</p></li><li><p>Nurture students into individuals of sound character with a sense of social responsibility</p></li><li><p>Enrich the character development journey of students in school and at home</p></li><li><p>Motivate and recognise students for demonstrating the school values</p></li></ul><p>The VIP Programme encompasses different tasks for students' completion and can be found in the Student Handbook. Teachers will endorse students' completion of tasks in the school with VIP stickers while parents acknowledge the completion of VIP (Home).</p><p>Students who have achieved Level 4 (at least 19 value points) will be rewarded with VIP stickers and HAF certificates in Term 4.</p><p><strong><em>&nbsp;</em></strong></p><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Screenshot__487_.png"></div><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Screenshot__486_.png"></div><p></p><div class="isomer-image-wrapper"><img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Screenshot__485_.png"></div><p></p>
