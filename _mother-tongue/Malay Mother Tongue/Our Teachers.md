# **Our ML Teachers**

![](/images/Curriculum/Mother%20Tongue/Malay/DeptGoal.jpg)

#### <u>Department Goal</u>

To develop a generation of confident and effective Malay Language anchored in values.

#### <u>Department Purpose</u>

To provide a positive and culturally vibrant environment for students to develop a love and appreciation for the language.
