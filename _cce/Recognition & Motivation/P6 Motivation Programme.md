# **P6 Motivation Programme**

The Primary 6 Motivation Programme is a series of compact workshops spread across Terms 1 to 3. It is aimed to support our primary six pupils by providing them with the requisite skills and mental resilience training so that they can be better motivated and equipped to handle the stresses of being a PSLE candidate.
