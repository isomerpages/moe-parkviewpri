<ol start="2" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Loan Policy</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Pupils are entitled to borrow <strong><u>four</u></strong> books at a time:
2 English and 2 Mother Tongue books.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The loan period for books is <strong><u>14 days</u></strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils must produce their EZ-link card or photocopy of it if they wish
to borrow books from the library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils have to return the books by depositing them at the book drop or
personally at Circulation Counter.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils must not allow their friends to use their EZ-link card to borrow
books or other library materials.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils may borrow books during their scheduled library periods, during
recess or after school during the library opening hours.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="3" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Renewal Policy</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Books can be renewed for <strong><u>another 14 days</u></strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Each book can only be renewed <strong><u>once</u></strong>.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils may renew an item if:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>The item has not been reserved by another member.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The item has not been renewed before.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>The item is not overdue.</p>
</li>
</ul>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="4" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Lost / Damaged Library items</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>For every item lost/damaged, the pupil will have to purchase the same
titled book as a replacement.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>If the book is no longer available in the market, pupil will have to replace
a new book of equivalent value to the library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Cash payment is <strong><u>not</u></strong> accepted.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="5" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Overdue Books Policy</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Although there are no fines for overdue library books, all pupils are
expected to return the library books before the due date.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils who do not return their book(s) on the due date will not be allowed
to borrow any books until the overdue book(s) is/are returned.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<ol start="6" data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Library Etiquette</strong>
</p>
</li>
</ol>
<p>Pupils are expected to observe the following library etiquette:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Speak softly at all times.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Handle all the library materials with care.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Return the books to the shelves after reading them and place all items
in their proper places.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Only pupils from Park View Primary School are allowed in the library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Pupils who behave inappropriately will be asked to leave.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Eating or drinking is not allowed in the library.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School bags are to be placed outside the library before entering.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Wallets must be always kept with pupils.</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Usage of iPads in the library is permitted exclusively under the direct
supervision of teachers or librarian.</p>
</li>
</ul>

through the existing school library resources and find the specific item
