<h4>Student Programmes</h4>
<p>From Primary 1 to Primary 6, our students receive training to gradually
build their Basic ICT skills in accordance with the MOE Baseline ICT Standards
(New Media Literacies). Additionally, students experience subject-specific
ICT enriched learning experiences to prepare them as Future-Ready Digital
Learners.</p>

<h4>Staff Development</h4>
<p>With the introduction of SkillsFuture for Educators: e-Pedagogy in 2019,
the experience of Full Home-Based Learning (HBL), and Blended Learning
in 2020. The school believes in developing our staff to leverage technology
for teaching and learning as a critical driver to attain its intended student
outcomes.
<br>
<br><strong>ICT Department used the following learning platforms to build staff capacity in e-pedagogy:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>School wide Professional Development on e-pedagogy</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Regular ICT sharing sessions conducted by ICT Champions; 15 min Tech &amp;
Go</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>ICT Mentoring sessions by ICT Mentors</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Courses conducted by AST, ETD or other organisations</p>
</li>
</ul>
