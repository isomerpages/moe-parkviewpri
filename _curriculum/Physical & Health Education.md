<h4><strong>Department Goal</strong></h4>
<p><strong>Vision</strong>
<br>To develop students who have the skills, values, and confidence to enjoy
a lifetime of active and healthy living.</p>
<p><strong>Mission</strong>
<br>To build strong foundations in fundamental movement skills (FMS) and inspire
students to remain active beyond their schooling years through enjoyable
and meaningful physical activities.</p>
<p><strong>Framework</strong>
</p>
<p></p>
<div class="isomer-image-wrapper">
<img style="width: 100%" height="auto" width="100%" alt="" src="/images/Curriculum/PE new/PE_Framework.png">
</div>
<p>The Physical Education programme is guided by two key aspirations: Sports
for All and Sports Excellence. Together, these reflect our commitment to
ensuring inclusive participation while providing opportunities for students
to strive towards higher levels of achievement.</p>
<p>This is operationalised through three interconnected pillars: Enrich,
Support, and Stretch, which collectively provide a balanced and holistic
approach to student development.</p>
<p></p>
<h4><strong>🔵 Enrich (PE Curriculum)</strong></h4>
<p>The <em>Enrich</em> pillar focuses on providing all students with broad-based
exposure to physical activities through a structured and progressive Physical
Education curriculum. Emphasis is placed on the development of fundamental
movement skills, physical competence, and the cultivation of positive attitudes
towards active living.</p>
<p><strong>Highlights:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>P1 Jump Rope Enrichment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P2 Wushu Enrichment</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Progressive PE lessons</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>⚫ Support (Character Education)</strong></h4>
<p>The <em>Support</em> pillar emphasises the holistic development of students
by nurturing values, character, and social-emotional competencies within
a safe and supportive environment. Through intentional design and reflection,
students develop resilience, teamwork, responsibility, and respect.</p>
<p><strong>Highlights:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>Character and Values Education in PE</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>PE Holistic Development Profile (HDP)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P4 Outdoor Learning Trip</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>P5 Outdoor Adventure Learning Camp</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Daily Unstructured Play (3Is: Intentional, Inclusive, Inspiring)</p>
</li>
</ul>
<h4><strong>🔴 Stretch (Sports Programmes)</strong></h4>
<p>The <em>Stretch</em> pillar provides opportunities for students to extend
their abilities, deepen their competencies, and pursue excellence in sports.
Students are encouraged to challenge themselves, demonstrate discipline,
and strive for personal and team achievements.</p>
<p><strong>Highlights:</strong>
</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p>PVPS Olympics</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>Learning for Life Programme (Sports Experiential Programme)</p>
</li>
<li>
<p>School-based sports events and competitions such as P1 Tele-match and
P6 Games Day</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Whole School Experiences</strong>
</p>
<p>To further reinforce the development of active and healthy lifestyles,
the department implements whole-school initiatives that complement the
three pillars:</p>
<ul data-tight="true" class="tight">
<li>
<p><strong>Active Friday</strong>, which promotes regular participation in
physical activity through structured school-wide programmes;</p>
</li>
<li>
<p><strong>Health Awareness Week</strong>, which builds students’ awareness
and understanding of healthy living through activities such as the Health
Plate Competition and partnerships with external organisations.</p>
</li>
</ul>
<p><strong>Our Commitment</strong>
</p>
<p>Through the integrated approach of <strong>Enrich, Support, and Stretch</strong>,
the Physical Education programme seeks to develop students who are physically
competent, resilient in character, and motivated to lead active and healthy
lives. This framework ensures that every student is supported in their
journey from participation to personal growth and, where appropriate, towards
excellence.</p>
